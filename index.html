March is Fire Prevention Month! However, fires may happen anywhere at any time, regardless of the month. It is incredibly
easy for a house fire to happen, but it is also not too difficult to prevent one. Here are some ways you can prevent a fire from happening
in your home.



HOW TO PREVENT A HOUSE FIRE
Install smoke alarms on every level of your home, especially inside bedrooms and outside areas where people sleep.
Test your smoke alarms every month. If theyâ€™re not working, change their batteries.
Discuss a fire escape plan together with the whole family, and practice the plan twice a year.
Unplug items that aren't in use. Overheating and the surge of electricity could be sources for a fire.
Do not keep cooking appliances unattended when they are on and are being used.


Though there are a handful of ways to help lower the risk of a house fire, it is important to keep in mind that there is no
100% guaranteed way to prevent one. These methods used to prevent a house fire can only decrease the possibility
of a house fire, not completely stop one from happening. However, these would very much aid in avoiding a fire
from happening.
